About

I am a PhD candidate at the University of South Carolina under the supervision of Matthew Ballard and a member of the SCAGNT research group. Previously, I was a M.Sc. student at the University of New Mexico and was advised by Alexandru Buium. Much of my current work focuses on the application of derived categories to problems in both algebraic geometry and commutative algebra.
