Pat Lank

About

I am a PhD candidate in pure mathematics at the University of South Carolina under the supervision of Dr. Matthew Ballard and a member of the SCAGNT research group. This Spring 2024, I will be a Program Associate in April at the MSRI (now SLMath). Previously, I was a M.Sc. student at the University of New Mexico and was advised by Dr. Alexandru Buium. Much of my current work focuses on the application of derived categories to problems in both algebraic geometry and commutative algebra.
